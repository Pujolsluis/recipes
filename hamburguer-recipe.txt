Ingredients:
1- Meat
1- Hamburguer Bun
1- Tomato
1- Lettuce
1- Ketchup
1- HoneyMustard

Directions:

1. Pre-Heat Grill, grease it.
2. Cook the Meat to a well done state.
3. Add cheese on top of the Meat.
4. Add Bacon to the grill.
5. Open the bread and add the veggies.
6. Put the Meat with cheese on top of the veggies.
7. Add the bacon to the top of the meat.
8. Add Ketchup and HoneyMustard.
9. Close Bun and Enjoy.
